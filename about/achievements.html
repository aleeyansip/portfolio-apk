Cake Project: Sales and Profit Report

Our business has been actively managed and operated by 7 dedicated group members. During this selling period, we diversified our product offerings by introducing two main items: our signature Matcha Moist Cake (RM 8.00) and our premium Chocolate Moist Cake (RM 9.00).

Net Bank Balance

Overall, the business successfully generated a total gross sales revenue of RM 1,779.40. After deducting business material expenses of RM 520.00 (with an additional RM 40.00 covered out-of-pocket by the team at RM 5.71 per person), our business achieved a combined net bank balance of RM 1,259.40.

To ensure this report accurately reflects individual effort, payouts were calculated dynamically based on the specific number of units sold by each member:

  • Ihjaz recorded the highest sales performance, securing a personal total take-home return of RM 224.91.
  • The financial returns of the remaining group members varied according to their individual cake units sold, as detailed in the charts below.
  • Crucially, our primary group objective was met with outstanding success: not a single member received a personal return below RM 150.00.
