Problem Solving

Every business faces challenges. Here is how Sedap Cravings Pastry Chef overcame ours.

Challenge 1: Flavor Balance (Overly Sweet Cakes)

Many cakes currently available in the market are far too sweet for customers' preferences

Our Solution

To fix this, Sedap Cravings uses high-quality matcha powder that introduces an elegant, slightly bitter taste, which perfectly balances out the sweetness of the ganache

Challenge 2: Cleanliness and Packaging during Delivery

There is a risk of the ganache getting messy or ruined while being delivered around the campus hostels and faculties

Our Solution

The business uses airtight, transparent plastic containers to protect the cakes, ensuring that both the quality and the aesthetic presentation remain intact and attractive by the time they reach the customers

Support Our Student Journey

This business is a heartbeat of our APK project at UMS. Every order helps us learn, and every follow helps us grow.
