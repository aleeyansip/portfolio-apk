Vision, Mission & Objectives

The driving force behind Sedap Cravings Pastry Chef.

Our Vision

Our Company's vision is to become the preferred dessert brand of choice by offering unique, trending flavors through our distinct, modern, and widely loved matcha moist cake products, specifically targetting the student demographic, in line with our tagline: 'Hidup lebih manis dengan matcha.'

Our Mission

  • To provide high-quality, homemade Matcha Moist Cakes that cater to the exact tastes and preferences of our customers.
  • To offer budget-friendly, student-centric pricing without compromising on the premium quality of our products.
  • To produce desserts with a unique, modern taste that aligns with current culinary trends.
  • To ensure our products are easily accessible through a fast, practical, and seamless Cash-On-Delivery (COD) service.

Our Objectives

  • Objective 1: To capture a 20% market share of the local students population within the first six months of operations by executing targeted social media marketing campaigns and setting up strategic on-campus micro-distribution.
  • Objective 2: To maintain an order fulfillment rate where at least 95% of COD orders are successfully completed within 30 minutes of the scheduled time, maximizing customer convenience and dessert freshness.
  • Objective 3: To launch at least one limited-edition variant of moist cake, utilizing customer feedback metrics to continuously adapt to evolving dessert trends among youth.
